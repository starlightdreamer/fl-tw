About

I’m an enthusiast at the bench: cleaning, adjusting, and putting manual typewriters back into steady use.

I’ve sold a few serviced machines through Facebook Marketplace when something was ready to go—that’s also where listings and sale posts usually go first. Most of what I do is repair and restoration for people who want a machine they’ll actually type on.
